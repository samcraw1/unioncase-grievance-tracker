Install on Your Phone

1
iPhone (Safari) Open app in Safari → Tap Share button → "Add to Home Screen"
2
Android (Chrome) Open app in Chrome → Tap ⋮ menu → "Add to Home Screen"
The app will appear on your home screen like a regular app!

Create Your Account

1
Click "Register" on the login page
2
Enter your email and create a password
3
Select your role: Employee, Steward, or Representative
4
Enter your facility and craft

File a Grievance

1
Click "File New Grievance" on dashboard
2
Fill in incident details (date, time, location)
3
Select the contract article violated
4
Describe what happened
5
Submit - you'll get a grievance number

Upload Evidence

1
Open any grievance
2
Scroll to "Documents & Photos"
3
Take Photo - Opens camera directly
Upload - Select files from your device
Accepted files: PDF, JPG, PNG (max 10MB). Photos are automatically compressed.

Key Features

Track all your grievances
Automatic deadline alerts
Upload photos & documents
Export to PDF
Works on any device
Secure & private

Who Sees What?

Role Can See
